Paintings

Explore expressive painting styles using acrylics, watercolors, and mixed media. Artists will learn color theory, brush techniques, texture layering, blending, and emotional storytelling through guided and free-flow painting sessions.
Tools & Techniques: Acrylic & watercolor paints, brushes, palette knives, canvas work, layering, color mixing, and composition.

Clay & Sculpture

Transform raw materials into meaningful forms. This category focuses on shaping, carving, and building three-dimensional artwork while understanding balance, structure, and artistic intention.
Tools & Techniques: Air-dry & ceramic clay, sculpting tools, hand-building methods, texturing, smoothing, and form development.

Photography

Capture moments, moods, and stories through the lens. Learn how to frame powerful shots, work with natural and studio light, and enhance images through thoughtful editing.
Tools & Techniques: Digital cameras & smartphones, composition rules, lighting control, perspective, photo editing basics.
